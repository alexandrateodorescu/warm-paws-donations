Why Blankets Are Essential

Blankets play a crucial role in providing comfort and warmth to shelter animals. They help reduce stress, provide a sense of security, and create a more home-like environment for animals waiting for their forever homes!

Benefits of Blanket Donations

  • Provides warmth during cold weather
  • Creates a comfortable resting space
  • Helps reduce anxiety in shelter animals
  • Makes kennels more inviting for potential adopters
  • Can be used for bedding, enrichment, or medical recovery

How to Donate

We accept both new and gently used blankets. Please ensure they are:

  • Clean and in good condition
  • Free of holes or loose threads
  • Machine washable
  • Appropriate size for shelter animals
